Frequently Asked Questions

How does the daily budget work?

You start each day with 240 minutes of focus time. Each session you complete (or quit) deducts the planned duration from your budget. The budget resets automatically at midnight.

Can I change the daily budget?

Yes! Tap the Settings icon on the main screen to customize your daily budget (60–480 minutes). You can also use the Reset button to restore it to the default at any time.

What are presets?

Presets let you quickly select common session lengths (25, 50, or 90 minutes) with a single tap, instead of adjusting the duration manually each time.

How do I view my focus statistics?

Tap the "Stats" button to see your weekly focus summary, including total focus time, number of sessions, completion rate, and your current streak of consecutive focus days.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your session history and statistics will be permanently removed.

Why do I see ads?

MinimalFocusTimer is free to use. Ads help support continued development of the app. We keep ads minimal and non-intrusive to preserve your focus experience.
