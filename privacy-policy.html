Privacy Policy

MinimalFocusTimer

Overview

MinimalFocusTimer ("the App") is a focus timer application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All timer data, session history, and settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage only.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) and usage data for the purpose of serving personalized ads. This data is handled by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. No other third-party analytics or tracking services are used.

Notifications

The App uses local push notifications to alert you when a focus session is complete. Notification permissions are requested through the standard iOS permission system. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Data Storage

All data (remaining daily budget, session history, labels) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is not backed up to any cloud service by the App itself. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be removed.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
